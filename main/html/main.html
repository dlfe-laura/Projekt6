Who are we?

P6 is a politically and religiously independent student association that works with STI, HIV and pregnancy prevention as well as sexual and relationship issues. P6 target group is students at Lund University. Our goal is to gain greater awareness and safer sexual behavior among Lund's students. We also work to increase respect for different individuals' sexuality.

Join us

For our members, we arrange ongoing training opportunities, discussion meetings, film evenings and other pleasant member activities. The activities are based on the wishes of the members. If you want to become a member, you can become one by visiting our office, contacting us via e-mail or on our website.
