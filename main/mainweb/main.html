Who are we?

P6 is a politically and religiously independent student organization dedicated to promoting sexual health and well-being among students at Lund University. We focus on STI, HIV, and pregnancy prevention, as well as supporting students in navigating sexual and relationship-related topics. Our mission is to foster greater awareness, encourage safer sexual practices, and promote respect for diverse sexualities and identities within the student community.

Join us

For our members, we arrange ongoing training opportunities, discussion meetings, film evenings and other fun activities. The activities are based on the wishes of the members. If you want to become a member, you can become one by visiting our office, contacting us via e-mail or on our website.
