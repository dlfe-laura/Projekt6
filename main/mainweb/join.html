LGBTQ+

The LGBTQ+ committee promotes inclusion, support, and visibility for LGBTQ+ students. We organize events, raise awareness, advocate for equality, and provide safe spaces for community and dialogue. Through collaboration and education, we work to create a welcoming campus environment where all identities are respected and celebrated.
