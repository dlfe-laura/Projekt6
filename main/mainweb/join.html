Join us

Our committees are the heart of our organization, each dedicated to promoting sexual health, inclusivity, and student well-being. Whether you're passionate about advocacy, education, creating safe spaces, or just wanting to meet new people, there's a place for you in one of our committees!

Committees

LGBTQ+

The LGBTQ+ committee in the P6 student organization promotes inclusion, support, and visibility for LGBTQ+ students. We organize events, raise awareness, advocate for equality, and provide safe spaces for community and dialogue. Through collaboration and education, we work to create a welcoming campus environment where all identities are respected and celebrated.

Education

The Education Committee focuses on producing educational events and workshops. We do everything from Sex-Ed Pub Quizzes to Movie Nights to Sexy Fikas. In our committee we try to spread education about sexual health in a receptive way and create spaces where people can openly discuss and learn about sexual health.

Social Media

We Marketing and Social Media team are responsible for our online presence and the community. By sharing event information, behind the scenes or educational contents regarding sexual health in our own fun ways on the platforms like Instagram or Facebook, we try our best to make a difference!

Board

Chairperson

The Chairperson oversees the overall operations of Projekt6, ensuring that all committees and board members work together effectively. They lead meetings, represent the organization externally, and provide strategic direction to achieve its mission.

Treasurer

The Treasurer manages the organization's finances, including budgeting, tracking expenses, and ensuring financial transparency. They work closely with the board to allocate resources effectively and maintain financial stability.

Internal Affairs

The Head of Internal Communications at ProjektSex manages internal messaging, responds to emails, and shares key updates with committees. They attend board meetings, support event planning, and create the organisation's newsletter. Their role ensures smooth communication, team alignment, and a connected, informed community across all areas of the student sexual health organisation.
